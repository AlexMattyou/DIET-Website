About Us

Welcome to the District Institute of Education and Training (DIET) in Thoothukudi district, Tamil Nadu, India. We are a premier institution dedicated to providing quality teacher education and training to the students of the district.


Campus Details

Our History

Established in the year 1992-1993, DIET Thoothukudi has a rich history of serving the educational needs of the district. Our institution was born out of a vision to improve the quality of basic education and create a desirable environment within the district to enroll more children of school-going age and retain those who are admitted in schools. Over the years, we have grown and evolved to become a leading institution in teacher education, committed to providing innovative and effective training programs to our students.


Historical background

Our Mission

Our mission is to impart quality education in the district and to create a pool of trained and motivated teachers who can make a positive impact on the lives of students. We strive to provide a conducive learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and critical thinking. Our goal is to equip our students with the skills and knowledge necessary to become effective teachers and leaders in their respective fields.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a model institution for teacher education in the district, recognized for our excellence in training and innovation. We aim to create a community of teachers who are passionate about learning, committed to excellence, and dedicated to making a difference in the lives of their students.

Functions of DIET

  1. Training (Pre service and In service)
  2. Resource support (Pre-Primary to Higher Secondary, Non-Formal, NILP)
  3. Research ( Action Research, Research Project, Teachers Action research)
